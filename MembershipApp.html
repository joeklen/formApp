Renewal Fee Structure
Renewal payment of dues by current members will be made annually in September, being the anniversary month of CPHC.

New Member Fee Structure
New Members (per current By-Laws), joining the club after the annual renewal date (September 31) will pay a prorated amount of 75% for the first quarter rounded up, 50% for the second quarter rounded up, 25% for the third quarter rounded up, and within the fourth quarter will pay the full amount of membership up until the next years renewal date. Any member who joins CPHC under the reduced prorated rate but does not pay in full at the next scheduled annual renewal must pay the full membership amount before being considered for any Club benefits as stated within CPHC by-laws for the next calendar year.
